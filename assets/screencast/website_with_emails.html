I follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. Nearly all hosting companies do this. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not directly linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

The purpose of the information that’s logged is for administering and maintaining the site. Also it enables me to react appropriately in case of any malicious activity on the server.
